The CARE Framework

A proven structure for effective AI prompts

  • Context: Set the stage ‚Äî what's the situation?
  • Action: What do you want the AI to do?
  • Result: What outcome are you looking for?
  • Example: Show what good looks like (optional)

Example Prompt:

"I'm a small business owner [Context] preparing a social media strategy [Action] to increase engagement by 20% [Result]. Here's a past post that worked well: [Example shows format and tone]"
